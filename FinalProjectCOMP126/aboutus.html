Who We Are

Since Spring 2021, Lily's Salon has proudly served the greater San Francisco area by providing top of the line nail care from our renowned stylists. We have quickly grown to be one of the top rated salons in the Bay area, guaranteering a relaxing and professional experience at every appointment. Please feel free to contact us for questions, to schedule an appointment, or to leave a review.
